About AGILE

The Adolescent Girls Initiative for Learning and Empowerment (AGILE) is a 5-year project (2020 - 2025) of the Federal Government supported by the World Bank to improve Secondary Education opportunities for adolescent girls between the ages of 10 to 20 years in selected states in the country.

The implementing states are Borno, Ekiti, Kaduna, Kano, Katsina, Kebbi, and Plateau.
